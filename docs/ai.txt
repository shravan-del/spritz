Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad field of computer science focused on creating intelligent machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Key areas of AI include:
1. Machine Learning: Systems that can learn from data
2. Natural Language Processing: Understanding and generating human language
3. Computer Vision: Understanding and analyzing visual information
4. Robotics: Physical systems that can interact with the environment
5. Expert Systems: Programs that emulate decision-making of human experts

AI has numerous applications in:
- Healthcare: Disease diagnosis and treatment planning
- Finance: Fraud detection and algorithmic trading
- Transportation: Self-driving vehicles and traffic optimization
- Education: Personalized learning and automated grading
- Entertainment: Game AI and content recommendation
